
How to make boot.img and update for RK3288

step 1

    ./img_build.sh
    
step 2

    typing "ums mmc 0" and Enter in rk3288's uboot cmd line.
    like:
    =>ums mmc 0
    
step 3
    
    sudo ./ums_update.sh
    
step 4

    use "ctrl-c" to kill "ums mmc 0" in rk3288's uboot cmd line.
    typing "boot" and Enter in rk3288's uboot cmd line.
    now kernel is new.
